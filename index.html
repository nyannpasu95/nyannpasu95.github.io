Tap-to-click for Apple Magic Mouse

MagicTapper brings trackpad-style tap-to-click functionality to the Apple Magic Mouse. Enjoy a smoother, quieter, and more ergonomic experience by reducing the need for physical clicks.

  • Natural tap-to-click gestures
  • Reduces finger fatigue and noise
  • Lightweight and runs in the background
  • Compatible with macOS
