Frequently Asked Questions

How does MagicTapper work?

MagicTapper adds trackpad-style gestures to your Apple Magic Mouse. Simply tap the mouse surface to click instead of pressing the physical button. Double-tap and hold to drag items - just like on a MacBook trackpad.

Is MagicTapper compatible with my Mac?

MagicTapper works with all macOS versions and is compatible with Apple Magic Mouse (first and second generation). It requires minimal system resources and runs silently in the background.

Does it work like the MacBook trackpad?

Yes! MagicTapper brings the same intuitive tap gestures from MacBook trackpads to your Magic Mouse. Single tap to click, double-tap and hold to drag - making your Magic Mouse feel like a natural extension of your trackpad experience.

Is MagicTapper free?

Yes, MagicTapper is completely free to download and use. If you find it helpful, you can support development through Buy Me a Coffee, but it's entirely optional.

Will this reduce wear on my Magic Mouse?

Absolutely! By using tap gestures instead of physical clicks, you reduce mechanical wear on the mouse button, extend its lifespan, and enjoy a quieter working environment.

How do I install MagicTapper?

Simply download the ZIP file, extract it, and move MagicTapper to your Applications folder. Launch it and you're ready to go. No complex installation process required.

Can I customize the tap sensitivity?

The current version uses optimized default settings that work well for most users. Future versions may include customization options based on user feedback.
